Ubtan Detan Sunscreen Ingredients

Mamaearth Ubtan Detan Sunscreen is formulated with a blend of natural, effective ingredients that provide exfoliation, brightening, and sun protection. Each component is carefully selected for its skin-loving properties.

Key ingredients include:

  • Turmeric (Ubtan): Natural exfoliant that brightens skin and reduces inflammation.
  • Vitamin C: Powerful antioxidant for detan and collagen production.
  • Zinc Oxide: Mineral sunscreen that provides broad-spectrum protection.
  • Chickpea Flour: Gentle scrub for smooth, glowing skin.
  • Aloe Vera: Soothes and hydrates the skin.
  • Neem & Pomegranate: Antibacterial and nourishing extracts.

This toxin-free formula is free from parabens, sulfates, and synthetic fragrances, making it safe for sensitive skin. Keywords: natural ingredients, Ubtan ingredients, detan ingredients, sunscreen ingredients, plant-based skincare.
