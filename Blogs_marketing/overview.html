Ubtan Detan Sunscreen Overview

Mamaearth Ubtan Detan Sunscreen is a revolutionary skincare product that combines the ancient wisdom of Ubtan with modern detan technology and powerful sun protection. This natural sunscreen offers SPF 50+ protection while gently exfoliating and brightening the skin.

Key features include:

  • Ubtan Exfoliation: Traditional Indian scrub for removing dead skin cells and impurities.
  • Detan Action: Reduces tan and evens skin tone for a radiant complexion.
  • Sunscreen Protection: Broad-spectrum SPF 50+ to shield against UVA and UVB rays.
  • Natural Ingredients: Made with plant-based, toxin-free components suitable for all skin types.

How to Apply

Apply generously to clean, dry skin 15-20 minutes before sun exposure. Massage gently in circular motions for exfoliation. Reapply every 2 hours or after swimming/sweating. Suitable for face and body. For best results, use daily as part of your skincare routine.
